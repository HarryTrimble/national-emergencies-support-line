Out-of-hours and scaling down the support line

Victim Support will take calls out-of-hours, for example when British Red Cross is unable to staff the support line during this time or demand for the support line is minimal.

Calls will be diverted back to Victim Support at an agreed time, upon assessing the on-going:

  • Number of people calling
  • Types of support people call about
  • Unmet needs

During this time, a process will be put in place to refer people where support is best given by British Red Cross or Samaritans.
