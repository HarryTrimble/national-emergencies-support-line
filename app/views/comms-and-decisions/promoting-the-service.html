Promoting the support line

British Red Cross and Victim Support will decide when the support line should be promoted. This decision will be based on agreed criteria.

British Red Cross or Victim Support will recommend when to promote the support line. They will communicate this through official channels during an emergency.

Comms templates are available for promoting the support line.
