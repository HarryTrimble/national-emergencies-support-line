Decision making

Key decisions that are made when an emergency starts:

  • Should the service be promoted?
  • How the service should be promoted?
  • When should the service be promoted?
  • Which organisation will answer the calls?
  • When to scale down the service?

Decision will be informed by:

  • Scale of the emergency
  • Anticipated and on-going need
  • Number of calls recieved
  • Media coverage

Key decisions about the service are made by:

  • Chief Executive, Victim Support
  • Head of Crisis Response, British Red Cross

A select group of representatives from Victim Support, British Red Cross and Samaritans communicate using a National Emergencies Support Line WhatsApp group.
