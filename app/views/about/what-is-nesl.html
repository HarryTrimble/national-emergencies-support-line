What is the support line

National Emergencies Support Line is a free service for people to access support in emergencies. The support line is operated by Victim Support, British Red Cross and Samaritans.

The support line provides one single point of contact for the public when there is a serious incident or emergency.

The number is free to call and will be promoted as soon as an emergency is declared.

Those answering calls will deal directly with people's needs or swiftly signpost them to where to go for the support they need.
