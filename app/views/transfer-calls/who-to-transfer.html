Who to transfer

When it is time to promote the support line this is the criteria for transferring people.

  • Anyone who feels affected by the major emergency. For example, someone who witnessed a terrorist attack.
  • People who would benefit from the support and advice available through the support line. For example, someone who had to evacuate their home due to a major flood.
