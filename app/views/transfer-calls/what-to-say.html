What to say to your callers

When it is time to signpost people to the support line you can use this script to ask whether they want to be transferred to the support line.

Before we finish I want to tell you about the National Emergencies Support Line; a free and confidential service run by the British Red Cross and Victim Support.

The support line is completely independent from us. Any information shared with them won't be shared with us.

It is for people affected by [emergency-name] to get advice and support, including [support-example] and [support-example].

I can put you through now if you like?
