Service availability

The phone number automatically goes to Victim Support, who operate 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

Decision making

Key decisions about the service are made by:

  • Chief Executive, Victim Support
  • Head of Crisis Response, British Red Cross

A select group of representatives from Victim Support, British Red Cross and Samaritans communicate using a National Emergencies Support Line WhatsApp group.
