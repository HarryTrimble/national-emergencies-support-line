Scaling up the service

There will be times when service capacity will need to be increased, for example:

  • The organisation taking calls doesn't have enough staff available to take the number of calls
  • Another emergency starts
  • A large proportion of calls are from people wanting to access funds or donate money

In these situations a decision can be made to divert calls between Victim Support and British Red Cross to share demand, using a dual option answering system.
