National Emergencies Support Line is a free service for people access support in emergencies.

The service is operated by Victim Support, British Red Cross and Samaritans.

Call the free helpline:

{% include "includes/phone-number.html" %}

Available 24 hours a day, every day of the year.

Advice and support available through the service

Speak to trained adviser for support and advice, including: 

  • emotional support
  • where to get supplies like food and clothing
  • advice on getting shelter and temporary accommodation
  • advice on getting counselling services
  • advice to getting impartial insurance advice
  • advice on getting any financial support available
  • advice on evacuating your home
