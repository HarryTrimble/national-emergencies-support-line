About the service

National Emergencies Support Line is a free service for people access support in emergencies. The service is operated by Victim Support, British Red Cross and Samaritans.

{% include "includes/phone-number.html" %}

Advice and support available through the service

People in emergencies can speak to trained adviser for advice and support, including: 

  • Emotional support
  • Where to get supplies like food and clothing
  • Information on specialist services like finance, housing, mental health
  • Advice on getting any financial support available
  • Advice on evacuating their home
  • Planning their next steps
